Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an event which brings together people from various educational backgrounds to collaborate intensively on a high-quality solution for a given problem.

Q: How does a Virtual Hackathon work?

This hackathon will be held exclusively online using GitHub and Google Meet. There will be a general room where all the information sessions will be held as well as breakout rooms for the individual teams. Coaches and mentors will also be available during scheduled time frames where teams can pose questions.

Q: Hackathon! Sounds scary, I’m not a coder. Can I play?

Yes, you sure can. Not a problem! Normally, if not all, most people have zero experience in their first hack. It’s entirely irrelevant what your experience is going into a hackathon, it’s more about your interest in technology. Every Member should be passionate about making hackathons a great learning and memorable experience.

Q: What is the maximum number of participants in a team?

The teams will be limited to a maximum of 3 members.

Q: Will we receive any support from mentors/coaches during the hackathon?

Yes, mentors and coaches will be available during the event. There will be a schedule accessible on the general forum to where you can see the coach/mentor timeslots and availability.

Q: We’ve already started some development, can we join?

Yes, absolutely. We welcome all skill sets and all stages of development –from zero to running start-up. It’s our job to see how teams progress and judge fairly. Anyhow it will be great to have a mixed level of participants –
everyone can learn something new!

Q: What is the cost for the Hackathon?

Nada,zero!! We are trying very hard to provide everything you need for a great event. It’s totally free to participate
